# Thyself — Brand Positioning

**Tagline:** understand why you are the way you are.

## Core positioning

Thyself is the foundation layer of the self-awareness journey. Before therapy, before any real change, you need a map of your own psyche. Typology gives you that map.

Adjacent to mental health — not a clinical tool, not a party trick. A serious self-knowledge platform for people doing inner work.

## What Thyself delivers

Not transformation. Recognition. The moment you read your type description and think "how did they know that about me" — that's the product.

People don't need to be told they can change who they are at the core. They need to *understand* why they are the way they are. That understanding is what makes real growth possible.

## Who it's for

- People in therapy who want tools between sessions
- People trying to understand their patterns in relationships and work
- People who've always known something about themselves they couldn't name
- Anyone doing the inner work, at any stage

## Voice and tone

- Honest, not mystical
- Warm, not clinical
- Deep, not condescending
- No "you can fix yourself" — recognition, not prescription
- No AI branding, no oracle framing, no dashes in UI text

## What we're NOT

- Not a replacement for therapy
- Not a diagnosis tool
- Not pop-psychology ("ENFPs are so random lol")
- Not surface-level — we go into tritypes, instinctual subtypes, shadow functions

## Tagline variations

- **Full:** *finally understand why you are the way you are.*
- **Short:** *understand why you are the way you are.*
- **Ultra-short:** *know yourself.*

## For acquisitions / partnerships

Target: Truity, Integrative9, Enneagram Institute, Keirsey, mental wellness platforms wanting depth and credibility.

Differentiation: grounded in Ichazo, Naranjo, Riso-Hudson, Jung, Briggs Myers — not invented or conflated with pop-psych stereotypes.
