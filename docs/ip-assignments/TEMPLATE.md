## 1. Assignment of intellectual property

Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to Assignee all right, title, and interest in and to all Intellectual Property created by Assignor in connection with the Project, including but not limited to:

- All source code, algorithms, software, and related documentation
- All design assets, user interface elements, and visual materials
- All written content, copy, descriptions, and educational materials
- All inventions, discoveries, improvements, and innovations
- All copyrights, patents, trade secrets, and other proprietary rights

This assignment covers all work product created from _____________ [start date] to _____________ [end date], described as: _____________ [brief description of work].

---

## 2. Work made for hire

To the extent any work is considered "work made for hire" under applicable law, it is a work made for hire for Assignee. To the extent it is not, Assignor assigns all rights to Assignee under this agreement.

---

## 3. Moral rights waiver

Assignor waives any moral rights or equivalent rights in the assigned work to the maximum extent permitted by law.

---

## 4. Representations and warranties

Assignor represents and warrants that:
- Assignor is the sole author and owner of the assigned work
- The work does not infringe any third party's intellectual property rights
- Assignor has not previously assigned, licensed, or encumbered the work
- The work does not contain any open-source code in violation of any license

---

## 5. Cooperation

Assignor agrees to execute any additional documents and take any actions Assignee reasonably requests to perfect and record the assignment.
