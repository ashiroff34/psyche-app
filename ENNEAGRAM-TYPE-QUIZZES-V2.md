# Enneagram Quiz Bank V2

## Overview: Three Quiz Modes

This quiz bank supports three distinct modes of engagement:

### Track 1: "My Type" Daily Quiz
5 questions per day focused exclusively on the user's core Enneagram type. Difficulty progresses over 10 days (50 questions total per type). After completing their core type, the system advances to wing types. Days 1-2 cover Level 1 (basics), Days 3-4 cover Level 2 (wings & passions), Days 5-6 cover Level 3 (lines & groups), Days 7-8 cover Level 4 (subtypes), and Days 9-10 cover Level 5 (advanced integration).

### Track 2: "All Types" Daily Quiz
5 questions per day rotating through all 9 types with progressive difficulty. Days 1-9 cycle through each type at Difficulty 1, Days 10-18 cycle at Difficulty 2, and so on through Day 45. Days 46-90 feature mixed-difficulty cross-type comparisons.

### Full Quiz Mode
Available at `/enneagram/learn` — take all 50 questions for any type in a single session, no daily gating.

---

## Question Format Key

Each question includes:
- **Q[number]** — Sequential ID within the type
- **Difficulty** — 1 (easiest) to 5 (hardest)
- **Day** — Track 1 day assignment (1-50)
- **Track2Day** — Track 2 day assignment
- **Category** — Content category

# TYPE 1: THE REFORMER

## Difficulty 1 — Days 1-10 (Track 1) | Core Fundamentals

**Q1** | Difficulty: 1 | Day: 1 | Track2Day: 1 | Category: Core Fear
What is the core fear of a Type 1?
A) Being unloved or unwanted
B) Being corrupt, evil, or defective
C) Being insignificant or worthless
D) Being controlled by others
**Answer:** B — Type 1s fundamentally fear being morally flawed or corrupt. Their inner critic constantly monitors for imperfection as a defense against this fear.

**Q2** | Difficulty: 1 | Day: 1 | Track2Day: 1 | Category: Core Desire
What is the core desire of a Type 1?
A) To be admired and successful
B) To be safe and secure
C) To have integrity and be good
D) To be loved and appreciated
**Answer:** C — The Type 1's deepest desire is to be good, ethical, and to have personal integrity. Everything they do is filtered through this lens.

**Q3** | Difficulty: 1 | Day: 2 | Track2Day: 1 | Category: Core Motivation
What primarily motivates a Type 1 in their daily life?
A) The desire to be needed by others
B) The drive to improve things and correct what is wrong
C) The need to acquire knowledge and competence
D) The pursuit of new and exciting experiences
**Answer:** B — Ones are driven by an internal standard of rightness. They feel compelled to improve themselves, others, and their environment to align with how things "should" be.

**Q4** | Difficulty: 1 | Day: 3 | Track2Day: 1 | Category: Basic Description

A) Withdrawing from others to process emotions privately
B) Seeking approval through helpfulness and generosity

D) Adapting their personality to match what others expect
**Answer:** C — The hallmark of the One is the constant inner comparison between "what is" and "what should be," creating a running mental critique that drives their reforming behavior.

**Q5** | Difficulty: 1 | Day: 4 | Track2Day: 1 | Category: Basic Description

A) They ignore it to avoid conflict
B) They feel an internal compulsion to redo or correct it
C) They delegate it to someone more capable
D) They become emotionally overwhelmed
**Answer:** B — Ones experience an almost visceral need to correct errors. Leaving something done wrong feels physically uncomfortable to them, like an itch that must be scratched.

**Q6** | Difficulty: 1 | Day: 5 | Track2Day: 1 | Category: Basic Description
What is the inner experience of a Type 1 like on a daily basis?
A) A constant flow of creative ideas and emotional intensity
B) A persistent internal voice evaluating right from wrong
C) Anxiety about potential future dangers
D) A desire to connect deeply with every person they meet
**Answer:** B — Ones live with a relentless inner critic that judges their thoughts, actions, and surroundings. This voice is not optional — it runs continuously in the background.

**Q7** | Difficulty: 1 | Day: 6 | Track2Day: 1 | Category: Core Weakness
What is the core weakness (passion) of Type 1?
A) Pride
B) Envy
C) Anger
D) Fear
**Answer:** C — The passion of Type 1 is Anger, though it often manifests as suppressed resentment, irritation, or righteous indignation rather than overt rage, since Ones believe expressing anger is "wrong."

**Q8** | Difficulty: 1 | Day: 7 | Track2Day: 1 | Category: Core Weakness

B) Because anger violates their standard of being "good" and controlled

**Q9** | Difficulty: 1 | Day: 8 | Track2Day: 1 | Category: Type Identification
